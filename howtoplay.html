Rules of the Game

You are going to be using the keyboard to Jam out to your favorite instrument.

1. Selecting an Instrument [Press SPACE to hear]

You can navigate to the instrument selection page by pressing the space bar on the home page. Then, click the buttons or use the left and right arrow keys to select your choice among the drums, guitar, cello, and piano.

2. Playing an Instrument [Press ENTER to hear]

Once you have selected your instrument, you will be taken to the instrument page. On the drum page, you can play 9 different drum sounds. On the guitar, cello, and piano pages, you can play each note from a 12-note musical scale. Move through the different sound choices using the arrow keys, then press the space bar to hear what note or sound you are currently on, and press the enter key to hear it play!

Press BACKSPACE to return to homepage
